Detailed Description

Implements a trivial way to store and retrieve KWD metadata. May be subject to race conditions on extreme edge cases where multiple wakeword engines detect the same word, and both generate metadata, and only of impact should the metadata be of significant difference.

To be replaced by a more generalized audio data management system in later MAX Toolkit releases.
