Detailed Description

This class encapsulates all logic which may vary across partner implementations. Each major aspect of a multi-agent application is decoupled here, in similar spirit to the MAX Library interfaces. In particular:

  • Agent management
  • Audio management
  • Experience management
  • UI management

This separation of concerns is intended to benefit developers who may wish to add a new Agent or other element without needing to modify, or be concerned with, other parts of the application.
